GrowFood NGO

In this project we worked with GrowFood NGO to develop a web portal that tracks their work in the northern country area.

The web portal mapped their water, irrigation, animal rearing and agrovet service development projects.


BuildAPlace NGO

BuildAPlace NGO is a non-profit org that focuses on improving the infrastructure of areas.

Together we created a web portal that would track their school, road and health projects and how well distributed they are in the areas they work in.


Avian County

This county government needed a way to track their development record and share it with their consultants. The web portal showed their progress in achieving what they had promised in their manifesto.


GreenLand Group

This is an environmentalist group that actively works to improve the Birks country green living initiative.

We worked together and created a web portal that showed the forest cover and rivers within their country, this would help them improve measures to achieve their goals for their environment.
